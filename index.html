About Me

Data. Design. Dallas.

Hello! My name is Kalia Hayes and I'm an Information Technology and Systems senior with a concentration in Business Intelligence and Data Analytics at the University of Texas at Dallas. In my time at The University of Texas at Dallas, I've immersed myself in all things business and data driven by taking courses such as Web Development for Business Applications, Web Analytics, Database fundamentals, and Java OOP. I've also held memberships in various UTD clubs including Women in Business and Technology, Undergraduate Success Scholars, and the UI/UX Club.

Along with my UTD curriculum and extracurricular activities, I partake in various highly rated open online courses (thank you Coursera, Udacity, and EdX) around the web in order to increase my exposure to BI and data analytics. I also enjoy testing my growing abilities using open online databases and communities (another thank you to Data.world and Kaggle.com).

In addition to my studies, I've worked all throughout my UTD undergraduate career. Since my freshman year, I've worked for UTD as an inventory clerk and assist in updating and maintaining UTD's asset databases. I also currently work as a remote based social media manager for a various businesses and organizations. Through SMM, I have an created an environment where I utilize my business focused analytical skill set on a daily basis to drive sales and grow brand loyalty and awareness on various social media platforms. On top of my on-campus job, I had the amazing opportunity to intern with Interstate Batteries during summer 2018 following my Junior year. As the web development & content intern, I was able to fulfill a business need using skills gained from a long time passion of mine.

Apart from school and work, I enjoy live music, watching sports, singing, and my dog Jagger. I look forward to sharing much more about myself with you in the near future! Have a look at some of my abilities and projects below.
