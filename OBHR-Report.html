Jeff's Weekley Coffee // Organizational Design

My Organizational Behavior and Human Resources course took an integrated social science approach to enable students to better understand their work environments and the issues that arise from the complex interplay among organizational members. Course topics include: motivation, attitudes, ethics, communication, leadership, teamwork, power, negotiation, and culture .

Our end of the semester project tasked us with creating a startup company and forming a three year business plan, focusing specifically on designing the organizational component of the startup.  To successfully apply the concepts we covered in class and not just regurgitate the info, we had to answer the following questions, and then some:  How would you structure the business? What type of culture will you create? How will you manage performance, what will you reward? How will you attract and retain a high-performing workforce with the skills needed? How will you motivate and retain your current employees?  How will you measure employee engagement and why would this benefit you?
