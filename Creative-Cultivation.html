Creative Cultivation

Over summer '18 I interned at Interstate Batteries corporate office in the digital marketing department focusing mainly on front-end web development, marketing, and analytics. Our higher ups were on top of keeping us four marketing interns actively engage throughout the 10 week internship, so we were assigned additional side projects and presentations dealing with all things marketing.

Creative Cultivation was introduced to me as a fun, interactive, laid-back and intimate "chat" where us interns would present advertisement and marketing schemes that creatively inspired or otherwise impacted us. It was mid summer, the World Cup was still going on, and we were all feeling good with our internships and marketing crew, so as a group we chose to present on the topic of Leadership and Unity.
